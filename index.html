Chapter 04 / The Honest Part

What AI can't do. We'll tell you anyway.

No sports analytics tool is magic. Velocity AI is built on honesty about its limits — because that's the only way to build trust with the people using it.

LIMITATION 01

Algorithmic bias

Models trained on incomplete or biased historical data can reinforce existing inequalities. Velocity AI audits its training sets continuously and publishes what it finds.

LIMITATION 02

Overfitting

Algorithms that fit past data too tightly miss future reality. Injuries, coaching changes, and team dynamics don't live in a spreadsheet — and we don't pretend they do.

LIMITATION 03

Intangibles remain intangible

Leadership. Character. Team fit. These are the qualities that win championships, and no model can measure them. Velocity AI supports human judgment — it does not replace it.

Chapter 07 / Frequently Asked

Questions, plainly answered.

What exactly is Velocity AI?

A sports analytics platform that combines machine learning, biometric tracking, video analysis, and historical performance data into a single dashboard. Built for teams and athletes at every level — not just the top 1% who can afford enterprise analytics contracts.

When will I get access?

Estimated launch is 90 days after the campaign closes. Digital tier rewards (access credentials, onboarding materials) unlock on launch day. Team and organization tiers receive their onboarding calls within the first 30 days.

Who is this built for?

College and semi-pro teams, high school programs, individual athletes, coaches, scouts, sports journalists, and anyone serious about using data to improve athletic performance. You don't need a stats background — Velocity AI is built to be read, not decoded.

What makes Velocity AI different?

Three things. First, accessibility — pro-grade models at a price real teams can afford. Second, honesty — we publish our model limitations, not just our wins. Third, human-first design — we built the platform to support coaching judgment, not override it.

What happens if you exceed your funding goal?

Stretch goals include: expanding to additional sports (campaign launches with football, basketball, and soccer), a companion mobile app for individual athletes, and open-sourcing our bias-audit framework so the entire industry benefits.
